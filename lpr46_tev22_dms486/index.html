About PICcompose

An ECE 4760 Project by:

Diane Sutyak (dms486)
Tara van Nieuwstadt (tev22)
Laasya Renganathan (lpr46)

Composing music can be a time consuming task. It can be almost as difficult to notate the melodies as it is to find inspiration for them. When brainstorming ideas for tunes, oftentimes a composer will try it out on their instrument of choice, make notes, and then translate their thoughts into a music score editing software of their choice to generate the sheet music.

The goal of our project is to eliminate the middleman in the music composition process by creating a tool to directly generate sheet music after playing an instrument.
